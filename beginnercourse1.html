Lesson 2

Understanding the Components of a Rubik's Cube

Are you familiar with the Rubik's cube? It's a 3x3x3 puzzle made up of 21 pieces, including a central piece with three axles, 8 corner pieces with three colors, and 12 edge pieces with two colors.

Lesson 3: Understanding Rubik's Cube Notation

Why is it important to learn Rubik's Cube notation?

If you want to learn how to solve the Rubik's cube, it's important to familiarize yourself with the notation we use to describe movements of the cube. In this course, we'll only be using a handful of letters, but if you want to progress to more advanced techniques, you'll need to learn them all. Don't worry though, the notation is easy to pick up and you'll be able to perform all the algorithms in the beginner course with just a bit of practice.
